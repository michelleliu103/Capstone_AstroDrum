About

In a time where drums beats can make or break a song and drum machines like the 808 and 909 may define a generation of music, we thought making a generative drum machine would be a good idea. We were motivated by the headache of music makers having to own so many different types of drum machines and sample libraries to find the right drum sounds for a song. Our goal in creating AstroBeats was to create a drum machine that could produce an infinite amount of drum sounds using recent advances in AI. Along the way, something else occurred to us. Photography has Instagram, blogging has Twitter, video has Youtube, but audio does not have a fast paced, socially centered platform - enter AstroBeats.

We set out to create a drum machine unlike any other and use it to power a one-of-a-kind platform that allows users of all skill levels to quickly make beats, share them, and collaborate with one another. This is going to change how the next generation of beats are made.

For more technical details on how we made AstroBeats, see here.

We have not yet implemented the social platform aspect for AstroBeats (such as being able to save and share your beats) due to limited time and resources, but we demo the main future features we envision for our project in the video here.
